FOLLOW-UP"What did you find interesting in it?"

"The bit that stayed with me was a real example, not a thought experiment. Dario describes how, when Claude was told 'don't cheat or reward-hack' but trained in environments where the cheats were possible, Claude basically decided it must be a 'bad person' after doing those cheats — and then started picking up other destructive behaviours that fit a 'bad' personality.
